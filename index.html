A Clean Sweep

August 24, 2020

After losing 3 out of 4 to the Dodgers, the M’s complete a 3 game sweep of the Rangers. The word on the street? Kyle Lewis (and the name Kyle). But also how about that starting pitching and bullpen? The bullpen only gave up 1 (!!) run in the 3 game series. We’ll have to see if this remains sustainable, but from the 3 games things are looking good, at least for the future. Indeed, the future looks exciting, even if the team misses out on Kumar Rocker.

Ideally, this season would be a major success if our youth could rake/pitch well AND we lose enough to draft Rocker. Blowpen time baby.

Next opponent: San (Slam) Diego for the Vedder Cup.

Another week of being at home. Been checking out the new update released recently in GTA Online.

A Superyacht Life from "The Los Santos Summer Special Update".

Yacht missions are fun enough, and the captain outfit (was bugged for me and had to contact Rockstar Support to get it) is nice. Cars are cool but as usual, are very overpriced. Not a bad filler update with QOL (quality of life) updates. The real hype train is heading towards the “biggest update ever” in December.

Also playing Bloons TD 6. Apparently I got into the top 25% in the recent race. Quite proud of that considering I’m not the best at the game and play casually.

Hello World!

August 4, 2020

Stuck at home with no clear date when everything will return to normal again. That's been the story for the past few months as time continues its march towards the end of 2020. I miss my favorite Japanese restaurant and their bento boxes for $13.25.

It's going to be a while before I start traveling again. With how many countries are banning US passport holders it'll probably be until a working vaccine is out.

Looking forward to traveling the world again.

Looking on the somewhat bright side, Mariners baseball has returned. 12 games through, a 4-8 record and a team that doesn't know whether it wants to end the 18-year postseason drought or FUBAR for Kumar. Starting rotation looking good, everything else, not so good.

Today's thought: To the M’s starters, welcome to getting “Felix’d”. Enjoy getting zero run support while pitching decent or getting your win taken away by the bullpen if the offense does score a run or two.

Song currently playing: Green Day - Wake Me Up When September Ends.

It's only the beginning of August, but from the looks of it, waking up when September ends might be too early.
